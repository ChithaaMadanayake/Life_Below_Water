About Me

Hi! I’m Bella , a passionate Marine Conservationist dedicated to protecting and restoring our oceans. Growing up near the coast, I’ve always felt a deep connection to the sea—whether it was snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs or watching sea turtles hatch on the shore. That childhood fascination turned into a lifelong mission to conserve marine ecosystems and advocate for sustainable ocean practices. With experience in coral reef restoration, ocean cleanup initiatives, and marine wildlife conservation, I actively work to reduce plastic pollution, protect endangered species, and educate communities on the importance of marine sustainability. When I’m not diving into conservation projects, you’ll find me exploring new coastal destinations, capturing underwater photography, or simply enjoying the peaceful rhythm of the waves. Let’s work together to create a future where our oceans thrive!
